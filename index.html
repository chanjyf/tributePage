Alexi Laiho Life

  • 1979 - Alexi Laiho born in 8 April 1979.
  • 1993 - Laiho formed Children of Bodom with the drummer Jaska Raatikainen.
  • 2004 - Ranked No 96 ou of 100 Greatest Heavy Metal Guitarists of All Time by Guitar World.
  • 2004 - At the same year he ranked No 41 out of 50 fastest guitarists.
  • 2008 - The most special year for Alexi Laiho, he received the Dimebag Award for Best Shredder, an award from Metal Hammer Golden Gods.
  • 2019 - Last show with the Children of Bodom on 15 December 2019.
  • 2020 - He died at 29 December 2020, the press was released on Jan 4th, but was later confirmed at December 29th.
